--- layout: post title: Reading - Best of The Month #1 category: experiment ---
  • The New New Thing - Michael Lewis

    I'm a real fan of Michael Lewis. His storytelling is fabulous. This story is about Jim Clark, the founder of Silicon Graphics and Netscape.

  • Flash Boys - Michael Lewis

    Another M. Lewis. High-frequency trading at the Wall Street.

  • The Obstacle Is The Way - Ryan Holiday

    My introduction to stoicism. Ryan does a great job explaining how known philosophers like Marcus Aurelius or Epictetus saw the world.

  • Hooked - Nir Eyal

    Nir writes about the process of creating habit-forming products. If you're wondering why services like Facebook or Twitter are such successes: read this one.

  • The Kingkiller Chronicle - Patrick Rothfuss

    In contrast to the other four books, this one is a classic fantasy book. A cycle consisting of 3 major parts. The last one is yet to come out, but the first 3 books (part two is split up) are great. His language and style of writing are just fun to read.
